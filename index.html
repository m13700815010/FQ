About Ocean Guardians

We are a global community dedicated to protecting marine life and preserving our oceans for future generations.

Our Mission

Ocean Guardians is committed to creating a sustainable future by protecting marine ecosystems, reducing plastic pollution, and promoting responsible fishing practices worldwide.

Founded in 2012, we've grown from a small coastal organization to an international network with over 40,000 volunteers across 90 countries.

Our approach combines scientific research, community engagement, and policy advocacy to address the most pressing challenges facing our oceans today.

Our Impact

See how our efforts are making a tangible difference for ocean conservation.

Plastic Reduction

We've helped reduce plastic waste in oceans by over 15% in our target regions through cleanup and recycling programs.

Protected Areas

Established 28 marine protected areas covering over 2.5 million square kilometers of ocean habitat.

Education Reach

Educated over 500,000 students and community members about marine conservation and sustainable practices.

Coral Restoration

Restored over 12,000 square meters of coral reefs using innovative techniques that increase survival rates.

Species Protection

Implemented conservation programs that have increased populations of endangered marine species by up to 30%.

Clean Water Initiatives

Improved water quality in 45 coastal communities through sustainable wastewater management programs.
